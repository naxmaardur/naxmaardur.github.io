Games Models Mods

Magic of vile darkness

This mod project expands the amount of undead creatures in Skyrim to have more variety. The other objective for this mod was for me to learn how to convert models to Skyrim requiring me to learn how to rescale and rig. This together with Worn undead are two of my most downloaded mods that I made completely alone.

Downloads: 213.9k

link to page

Worn undead

Worn undead is a continuation of the mod above but seperated into another project because this mod adds undead versions for characters that already exist in the game but did not already have undead versions. For this project I modified the models of the base game to give all of them 6 to 9 different undead version coming to a total of 12 versions per character if we include its sister mod battle worn creatures. The models I made for this mod have also been used in another mod I made together with jayserpa called Immersive Death Cycle which has gotten 419K downloads.

Downloads: 211.9k

Nax ruins - Bleak falls barrow

This is the first of my Nax ruins mods, which are mods I make from time to time in which I try to redesign Skyrim ruins/dungeons to better fit their lore, make them more unique and replayable. My primary focus with this redesign is to create a distinct differance between the temple section and when it becomes the burial chamber. I also tried to remove skyrims shortcut at the end of the dungeon trick and made then dungeon slightly change and spawn new enemies to make it feel like the undead are trying to prevent you from escaping with the dragon stone.

link to page

Nax ruins - Ranveigs fast

Another dungeon redesign mod. Ranveigs fast is a very forgettable location in de base game of Skyrim because it's a very generic dungeon with only one side quest and almost no content in it. I tired to improve it by increasing the prominence of this dungeon adding more various ways into it connected to other parts of the surrounding map. I also added a whole new sub dungeon area which is supposed to be a continuation of another forgotten side quest. This new dungeon section took heavy inspiration from how the dark souls 3 catacombs look and feel.
