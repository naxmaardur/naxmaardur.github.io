MIA VREVEN

Game Designer

I have been fascinated with games ever since playing Spyro and Rayman. I have always wanted to work on games and eventually make my own game, I started making mods and experimenting with game engines from the age of 14 and moved on from there to actually studying it. My passion for game design lies in creating experiences that stay with players long after they have put down the controller. I strive to push the boundaries of storytelling and immersion in gaming, constantly seeking new ways to engage and captivate audiences, but still taking inspiration from games that have done this for me.

I started studying game development in 2017 and in 2022 I started to study design. My goal is to continue honing my skills and knowledge in game design and all other fields of games, with the ultimate dream of creating a game that leaves a lasting impact on players worldwide. I am excited to see where my journey in the gaming industry will take me.
