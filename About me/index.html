MIA VREVEN

Game Designer

I have been fascinated with games ever since playing Spyro and Rayman. I have always wanted to work on games and eventually make my own games. I started making mods and experimenting with game engines from the age of 14 and moved on from there to actually studying it. My passion for games lies in creating experiences that stay with players long after they have stopped playing.

I started studying game development in 2017 and got my degree in 2020. In 2022, I started studying design. My goal is to continue honing my skills and knowledge in game design and all other fields of games, with the ultimate dream of creating a game that leaves a lasting impact on players worldwide. I am excited to see where my journey in the gaming industry will take me.
