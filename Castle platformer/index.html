Castle Platformer

For this platformed game I focused on how the game felt to play, there are still some things that are not exactly how I wanted it mainly the normal movement needs some tweaking, but over all the project achieved what I set out for. To improve on the games feel I made almost every single set piece in the game react in some way to player interaction. I did this to make the player character feel like something that is in the world rather than some separate object that is moving though it. I also wanted to make the level design speak for itself at much as possible allowing players to discover the games rules and limitations by themselves. This worked for the most part but some things where a little to unclear, mainly a part later in the game where player have jump onto the dead knights that came before you, most players don't realize that they can stand on them without dying a few times first.

mechanics

The platformed has several mechanics to allow for a large amount of freedom in how players go through the levels. players can, sprint, ledge grab, ground pound into a high jump and attack spikes to bounce off them.
all of these mechanics together give the player a large amount of freedom in how to tackle the levels, but they are quite a lot for players to remember for the short length of the prototype.
